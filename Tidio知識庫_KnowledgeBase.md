## Materials / 材質

**Q: What materials do you offer?**
A: Two: premium acrylic and solid surface. We craft both in-house and help you choose the right one for your project.

**Q: Acrylic or solid surface — which should I choose?**
A: Choose acrylic for an upper-floor bathroom, flowing organic curves, a warmer first touch and a friendlier budget (27–45 kg — two-person install, no floor reinforcement). Choose solid surface for a ground-floor statement piece, a dense stone-like feel, long spa-style soaks and invisible repairs (90–150 kg — we advise on floor loading). Send us your floor plan and we'll recommend honestly — we make both.

**Q: How heavy are the tubs?**
A: Acrylic: 27–45 kg (two people can carry it upstairs; sits on standard floors). Solid surface: 90–150 kg (usually ground-floor placement or a floor-loading check; professional handling on delivery).

**Q: Is acrylic durable? What about scratches?**
A: Yes — acrylic tubs last decades with simple care. Surface scratches can be polished or filled and blended. Solid surface can be sanded and re-polished back to factory-new; repairs are effectively invisible.

**Q: Do you make copper or stone resin tubs?**
A: No — we specialise in premium acrylic and solid surface, which cover the widest range of custom shapes and real-world bathrooms.

## Sizes & customisation / 尺寸與客製

**Q: What sizes can you make?**
A: Length 1200–2000 mm, width 650–1000 mm, height 450–780 mm — including Japanese deep-soaking proportions. Outside these ranges, ask us — bespoke tooling is what we do.

**Q: Can you make an irregular / freeform shape?**
A: Yes. Our design studio has a Freeform Organic mode (asymmetric ends, waist pinch, organic lobes), and for fully irregular one-off forms just upload your sketch or CAD — we build truly irregular pieces.

**Q: What colours and finishes are available?**
A: Gloss white, matte black, gold leaf, stone/marble look — or a custom colour-match to any colour you like.

**Q: What add-ons are available?**
A: Slotted overflow, freestanding filler tap, chromotherapy LED (rainbow cycle or any fixed colour), air massage jets, and an integrated moulded headrest.

**Q: Will a 75 kg / large person fit comfortably?**
A: That's exactly what custom sizing is for — we shape length, width, depth and ergonomics around the bather. Tell us height/build and we'll recommend dimensions.
