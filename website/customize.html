✏️ Sketch Your Bathtub Shape

Draw a one-of-a-kind bathtub — sketch lines are auto-smoothed into clean curves and built into a 3D model. All dimension parameters remain adjustable afterwards.

① Top-View Rim Shape (required)

The rim outline seen from directly above. Draw a closed shape (ends auto-connect). You can also photograph a paper sketch and upload it (dark pen, white paper, even lighting).

② Side Wall Profile (optional)

Dashed line on the left = tub centerline. Draw one side wall curve from bottom to top (e.g. tapered base, bulging middle). If skipped, the "Base taper" slider is used instead.
