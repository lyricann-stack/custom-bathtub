No fine print surprises

What it costs, and what's included.

Typical price

Custom acrylic from AUD $2,990 — most projects land between $3,500 and $8,000. Solid surface from about $4,500. Your design studio estimate updates live as you size it.

The $399 design fee

Includes the photoreal render, the signed spec sheet and 2–3 refinement rounds — and it's fully credited to your order. Don't proceed? The files are yours to keep.

Payment & financing

Payment milestones are confirmed on your quote before production begins. Afterpay and Zip financing from around AUD $130/month on a $4,000 tub.

Delivery & installation

Crated, insured freight across Australia is quoted up front. Acrylic is a comfortable two-person lift on standard floors — any licensed plumber can complete the install.

Warranty

A 10-year structural warranty on acrylic and 15-year on solid surface, backed by Australian-based after-sales service.

Returns on a custom piece

You sign off the spec before the mould opens, so wrong-size returns don't happen. Anything damaged or out of spec is repaired or remade under warranty.
