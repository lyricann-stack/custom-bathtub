Before you ask

Questions homeowners ask.

How much does a custom bathtub cost?

Every custom bathtub — acrylic or solid surface — is USD $990, plus shipping quoted by your destination. You see the price as you build in the design studio — before you talk to anyone. Pick your destination in the design studio to see a live shipping estimate and total.

How long does it take?

48 hours to your photoreal 3D render, 4–6 weeks in production, then insured freight — typically 2–6 weeks depending on your destination, with progress photos along the way.

Is the $399 design fee refundable?

It is fully credited to your order, and 2–3 refinement rounds are included. If you decide not to proceed, the render and spec sheet are yours to keep.

What about delivery and installation?

We ship crated and insured worldwide from our factories in China & Thailand. Acrylic tubs are a comfortable two-person lift and sit on standard floors without reinforcement — any licensed plumber can complete the install. Solid surface pieces are heavier (90–150 kg) — we confirm access and floor loading with you before dispatch.

What warranty do I get?

A 10-year structural warranty on acrylic and 15-year on solid surface, backed by our factory-direct after-sales team.

Can I return a custom tub?

Because every tub is built to a spec you sign off before production, wrong-size returns don't happen. If anything arrives damaged or out of spec, we repair or remake it under warranty.
